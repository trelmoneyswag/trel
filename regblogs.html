June 26 (why is the measure of love loss?)

Today is my third day of singledom in a really really long time. Today I opened my favorite book (written on the body by Jeanette Winterson) and this is the opening line. I think I may embroidery something like this. I’m thinking a lot about love, and losing, and loving and losing. I also made my bed this morning, and made myself breakfast, and am drinking a two-day old latte. There’s something beautiful in the stability. I am loving in that I am existing, because I don’t know any other way to exist. I love in my actions and throughout my day. I don’t really feel the need to find myself. I know who I am, I have found comfort in myself especially in the past 2 or so years. I do, however, want to find new ways to love. I want to expand my understanding of love beyond just romance. I have too often viewed myself and my love as too overwhelming to give to people unless they explicitly agree to be romantically involved. No more!!! I’m excited to love my friends deeply and endlessly. I am myself in love and I am also in love with the world. How do I recommit myself to that love? How do I commit myself to change?
